Antica Corte Pallavicina

Ceremony
24th September 2016, 4pm
We are getting married at Chiesetta del Po, a small church on the banks of the River Po dedicated to the Blessed Virgin of Loreto. Above the altar there is an ancient image of the Madonna, which an unknown painter painted as an act of devout homage to the Virgin protector of the area from floods. The church also features a bell tower, which is an exact replica of the famous Torrazzo of Cremona.

The ceremony starts at 4pm, which is later than the usual English wedding ceremonies, in line with Italian tradition. You can park at our reception venue, the location of which is shown on the map pictured, which is a two minute walk from the church down a private drive.

Reception
